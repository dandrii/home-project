What is a dream house and what difference from a house with a "good repair"?
The dream house reflects the essence of its owners, is their continuation, supports them, gives them energy. It has a soul and a heart. He is alive!
My recipe for creating your dream home: first we create a foundation, take our desires, inspiration and energy, add breath to space, mix with your unique style, a pleasant color scheme and decorate with useful and comfortable details. That's it ... The dream house is ready!

First ingredient

  • Think up your house yourself!
  • Visualize together your perfect day in your ideal home
  • Think about what energies you want to fill your home, and give them colours
  • From the energies and dreams go to specific examples

Second ingredient

  • Space - the defining and most important component of the house, the foundation of the foundations
  • The most actual for all time is simplicity
  • Pleasant to your eye color scheme

Third ingredient

  • Everything else is details: light, materials, furniture, textiles ... This is very important!
  • Think light scenarios for your home
  • When choosing materials, your basic criterion is simplicity, functionality and naturalness

Creating a house of your dreams is a long and fascinating process, in which you just need to fall in love and get pleasure from it.
